Rise Up 4 CS

I work as a research assistant for Rise Up 4 CS, a program which supports underrepresented students taking AP Computer Science Advanced and AP Computer Science Principles through tutoring, mentorship, and engaging computing experiences.

My Accomplishments

  • Updated interactive content in the CSAwesome ebook to include junit tests for active code practice problems, increased interactive material for practice free response questions, and added midterm tests with instant feedback.

  • Developed an asynchronous online training module for incoming mentors that was centered around positive reinforcement and culturally informed teaching.

  • Developed content for riseup4cs.org to provide information to potential mentees and their parents/guardians about the history, mission, and eligibility requirements of the program.

My Goals

As I begin my second year as a mentor for Rise Up 4 CS, I am thinking about the areas in which I still have a lot to learn. Facilitating help sessions over video conferencing is a challenge, as is making personal connections with each student who attends. I think it is important to set attainable goals for oneself that can be tracked over time. My goals for my second year with Rise Up 4 CS include learning personal fun facts about each student I interact with, finding ways to connect CS concepts with material from each student's unique life, and presenting myself as a learner as well as a leader - even leaders can make mistakes as long as they own up to them!
