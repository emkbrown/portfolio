My Mission

Education

All students, regardless of location, race, identity, socio-economic status, or ability, deserve an equitable education. I am committed to making technology more accessible to students, and developing technology that makes online learning effective.

Mentorship

I am a firm believer in the importance of personal relationships and connection in all industries. I aim to provide emotional, professional, and academic support to the coworkers and/or students I work with.

Social Good

Technology has incredible potential to help those who need it most, and I want to harness that potential to work directly with and for people to create a sustainable world we are proud to live in.
