About Me

I’m a junior at the University of Michigan’s School of Information studying UX Design and Computer Science.

My Mission

  • Education

    • All students, regardless of location, race, identity, socio-economic status, or ability, deserve an equitable education. Now more than ever, technology is an essential part of a well-rounded education, yet many students are left without. I am committed to making technology more accessible to students, and developing technology that makes online learning effective.

  • Mentorship

    • I am a firm believer in the importance of personal relationships and connection in all industries. I aim to provide emotional, professional, and academic support to the coworkers and/or students I work with.

  • Social Good

    • I want to make and use technology that contributes positively to the health and well-being of its users. Technology has incredible potential to help those who need it most, and I want to harness that potential to work directly with and for people to create a sustainable world we are proud to live in.

My Strengths

  • Empathy & Communication

    • I am attuned to the emotions, wants, and needs of those around me, and I am very driven by my desire to connect with others.

  • Strategy

    • I am skilled in the areas of effectively expressing information, finding patterns in data, and developing options and alternatives to solve problems.

  • Resorative

    • I am committed to finding and solving problems that arise in all areas of work, and am constantly looking to improve.
